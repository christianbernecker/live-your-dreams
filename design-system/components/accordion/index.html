Accordion

Professional accordion component for real estate applications.

Accordion Variants

Default Accordion

Property Details

This accordion contains detailed information about the property including specifications, features, and additional notes.

 Location Information

Detailed location information including neighborhood details, public transport connections, and nearby amenities.

Elevated Style

Financial Information

Purchase price, financing options, additional costs, and payment schedules.

 Legal Documents

Energy certificate, floor plans, ownership documents, and legal disclosures.

Glass Effect

Premium Features

Luxury amenities, premium finishes, and exclusive building features.

Real Estate Specific

Property Specifications

Room layout, square footage, ceiling height, and architectural details.

 Lead Information

Contact details, viewing preferences, and qualification status.

 Important Notice

Special conditions, restrictions, or important legal information.
